What is Snippet?

Snippet is a technique developed by me with the purpose to make you do stuff without the usual pain that people feel when they're studying. This method is backed up by a lot of scientific research on the field. it's been tested with people with ADHD and autism and has already been tested with multiple people, just pace yourself and enjoy the time spent.

Tips

  • Choose a task to accomplish during the period.
  • Its strongly recommended by the author to set a timer of 20 minutes, because it is not much time, and also a very sweet spot to work.
